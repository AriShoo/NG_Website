The Scottish Wildlife Trust is a charity that is dedicated to conservation of the Scottish natural environment and it's wildlife.
I've been given a brief by this charity to make a game.

  • The mobile game will be given the theme of 'Nature'.
  • It will be suitable for ages 5+
  • Any aspect of nature can be focused on.
  • Elements could be a focus, such as: wind, earth, fire, or rain.
  • Biological aspects like animals or plants.

    • The primary goal of the game is to engage and entertain and any education placed in the game must be non-intrusive.
